About Billz Industry.

Billz Industry is a luxury streetwear-brand curating proudly
authentic South African stories & that soft signature life.
The brand is a creative collective established in year 2021 by
founder Olwethu Bhila who is an artist, and Zukhanye Mthembu,
a stylist both from Port Elizabeth. They blend artistry and style
in order to deliver unique South African streetwear design solutions.

Why choose Billz Industry?

Dual creative perspective, having an artist and a stylist collaborate
to bring fresh, luxury streetwear designs.
Unique Blend that merges art and fashion for standout graphic designs.
Creative and innovative approach that tells proudly South African stories
through streetwear fashion. Offer tailered solutions, focusing on customers unique visual needs and preference.

Our mission and values.

Our mission as the brand is to create and deliver innovative, premium quality
and authentic luxury streetwear clothing, including customisation to our customers
prefrences.Billz Industry is a luxury streetwear brand that aim to make mzantsi great
again through garments, arts and style. Exceed our customers expectations and build long
lasting relationships.
